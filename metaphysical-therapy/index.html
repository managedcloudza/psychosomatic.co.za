Metaphysical Therapy

Every event in our lives is processed as a personal experience and stored in the vast memory bank of the unconscious mind. Accessing these memories—whether as they actually occurred or as we initially perceived and processed them—requires navigating the deeper layers of the mind, specifically the deep subconscious mind and the shallow unconscious mind, where the sum of all our experiences is stored.

However, accessing these deeper regions is no simple task. The conscious mind, guided by the superego, often acts as a gatekeeper, making it challenging—or even impossible—to delve into the roots of our issues quickly and effectively. This barrier is why many traditional methods struggle to uncover the origins of deeply ingrained emotional and mental patterns.

Sky of Mind’s Revolutionary Approach
Through its unique method of Trans-State Induction or Super-State Suspension, the Sky of Mind system bypasses the barriers of the superego, granting direct access to both the deep subconscious and shallow unconscious mind. This breakthrough process allows for an unfiltered exploration of the memory bank, enabling a profound reevaluation of our past experiences.

Once these memories are revisited, we can compare them as they truly occurred with the personalized narratives we created around them. This dual perspective not only helps us understand the objective reality of our experiences but also sheds light on how our subjective interpretations have shaped our emotions, behaviors, and beliefs.

The Benefits of True Access
This enhanced level of access unlocks transformative possibilities, including:

  • Memory Improvement: Enhanced ability to recall events and experiences in vivid, accurate detail.
  • Perspective Shift: A more balanced and objective understanding of past events, free from subjective bias.
  • Emotional Liberation: The ability to witness every angle of our life circumstances, not just our own perspective.

This process fosters profound forgiveness—both for ourselves and for others—not as a superficial act of willpower, but as an authentic release from the soul's deepest residence. By freeing ourselves from the weight of past experiences, we move beyond the cycle of "trying to let go" and step into true emotional liberation.

The result? Freedom from yesterday’s burdens and the promise of a new dawn. This is the transformative power of Metaphysical Therapy with the Sky of Mind system.
