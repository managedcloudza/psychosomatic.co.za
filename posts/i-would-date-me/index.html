I WOULD DATE ME!

Dr. Jules S Greenstein Published on April 7, 2025

Would you date yourself? Gender aside, I'm talking about personality, character and value systems. Although we are all born with a specific nature formed by our genetic code and infused with our own signature Psychoneuro-emotional design, there are aspects about our perceptions and behaviour that we can adjust. Life hits us all at some or other point, but we must and can choose to think and behave in a manner which is conducive both towards our own mental health and towards our "personal brand", the way in which we are perceived.

Yes, our personalities are either categorized as sociophillic or sociophobic, attractive or repulsive. Yes, our characters are formed by the value systems which we adopt as a result of our attractions to and repulsions of behavioural patters in society and specifically, behaviour within our social circles.

So before we can attract the best, albeit a friend or partner, we first need to become the best version of ourselves.
